Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy for Bahay Pares Tapsihan – Dasmariñas Online Ordering and Delivery System

Effective Date: December 2023

CHAPTER I: INTRODUCTION

Background of the Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") outlines how Bahay Pares Tapsihan – Dasmariñas ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects your personal information when you use our online ordering and delivery web application ("Service"). We are committed to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information.

CHAPTER II: INFORMATION WE COLLECT

User Information

To provide you with our online ordering and delivery services, we collect the following user information during registration and usage of our platform:

Staff, Customer, and Admin Information: We collect basic information such as your name, contact number, email address, and address to create and manage your account.

Transaction Information

To process your orders and payments, we collect the following transaction information:

Payment Information: If you choose to make online payments, we collect the reference number and the order ID.

Order Details: We collect information related to your orders, including the items ordered, order date and time, delivery address, and order status.

CHAPTER III: HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

Account Management: To create and manage user accounts for staff, customers, and admins.

Order Processing: To process and fulfill your orders, including order status updates.

Payment Processing: To facilitate payment for your orders.

Customer Support: To provide customer support and address inquiries or concerns.

Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve our services.

CHAPTER IV: SHARING YOUR INFORMATION

We do not share your personal information with third parties except in the following circumstances:

Service Providers: We may share information with trusted service providers who assist us in providing our services, such as payment processors. These service providers are obligated to protect your information.

Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information when required by law or to protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property, or that of our users.

CHAPTER V: SECURITY

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information, but no method of data transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

CHAPTER VI: YOUR RIGHTS AND CHOICES

You have the following rights and choices regarding your personal information:

Access and Correction: You can access and update your account information at any time.

Data Deletion: You can request the deletion of your account and associated data.

CHAPTER VII: CONTACT US

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests related to this Privacy Policy or your personal information, please contact us at bahayparestapsihan@gmail.com.

CHAPTER VIII: CHANGES TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. The most recent version will be posted on our web application.

By using our online ordering and delivery system, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

In the wake of social distancing measures, they have invested in building an online ordering system that offers convenience and accessibility.
